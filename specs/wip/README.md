# /specs/wip/

## Purpose
Work-in-progress VRDs (Validated Requirements Documents) being drafted by the Business Analyst.

Documents in this folder are **NOT ready for planning** - they are still being consolidated, clarified, or reviewed.

## Owner
Business Analyst

## Status
Files here have `-DRAFT` status and should NOT be used for epic creation.

## Naming Convention
```
[FeatureName]-v[N]-DRAFT.md
```

**Examples:**
- `User-Authentication-v1-DRAFT.md`
- `Payment-Integration-v2-DRAFT.md`

## Lifecycle
1. BA creates draft VRD here from intake documents
2. BA iterates and refines the VRD
3. Once complete and validated, BA moves to `/validated/` with `-VALIDATED` status
